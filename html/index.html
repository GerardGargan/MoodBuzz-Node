Avoid the 'McCallister' Meltdown!

Ever found yourself in a Kevin-like whirlwind of emotions?

Now you can gain a better understanding of your emotions with MoodBuzz!

Unleash the power to decipher your feelings and unravel triggers with our Home Alone-inspired emotion recording tool. Snap emotional moments and decode the effects of individual triggers on your emotional state throughout the day or week.

Frequently Asked Questions

There is no cost, MoodBuzz is totally free to use and has been developed as part of a University project at Queens University Belfast.

MoodBuzz has been designed to be responsive to all devices. You can access MoodBuzz through a mobile, tablet or desktop device by visiting the web page on the device. We do not have a native app for Android or iOS.

MoodBuzz has been developed by Gerard Gargan, a MSc student studying Software Development at Queens University Belfast. This web app has been built to satisfy the requirements of CSC7084 - Web Development Module.
